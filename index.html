Industries & Contexts

Real industrial environments where robotics, automation and AI adoption are evaluated and applied

Automotive

Complex manufacturing environments where scalability, flexibility and system integration are critical. Projects must deliver measurable productivity gains while adapting to frequent product and volume changes.

Pharma & Life Sciences

Highly regulated production contexts requiring compliant, reliable and repeatable automation solutions. Technology adoption must align with validation, quality and long-term operational robustness.

Logistics & Intralogistics

Material handling and fulfillment operations driven by throughput, adaptability and labor constraints. Robotics solutions are evaluated based on integration effort, resilience and scalability under real operating conditions.

Food, Beverage & Tobacco

High-volume, fast-changing production environments where hygiene, robustness and uptime are essential. Automation must perform consistently while coping with product variability and demanding operational cycles.

Metal & Machining

Precision manufacturing environments focused on bending, welding and machining processes. Robotics and automation are applied to ensure repeatability, quality and safety while handling heavy or complex components.

Plastics & Rubber

Production of plastic or rubber components where precision, cycle time and consistent quality are essential. Automation solutions help streamline molding, handling and assembly operations efficiently.
